## What this product does

- Helps you understand how LinkedIn actually works for job hunting, without fake gurus or hype
- Tells you what to fix in your LinkedIn profile, step by step, in plain language
- Helps you decide what to post (and what not to post) based on your goal
- Saves you time by removing random, conflicting LinkedIn advice
- Gives you a clear, focused LinkedIn strategy instead of confusion

---

## What this product does NOT do

- It does NOT guarantee a job, referrals, or money
- It does NOT post anything on LinkedIn for you
- It does NOT run ads, sell courses, or upsell anything
- It does NOT track or spy on other people’s LinkedIn accounts
- It does NOT work as a public website or shared platform

---

## Single-user assumption

This product is built for **one person at a time**.

There is **no login system**, **no accounts**, and **no multi-user support**.

You use it for yourself, and only for yourself.

---

## Data ownership rules

**Where data lives**

All data stays with you on your own setup.

**Who can access it**

Only you. No one else can see, collect, or reuse your data.

---

## Non-goals

Things we are consciously not building:

- A SaaS product or startup business
- A “make money fast” LinkedIn hack tool
- A generic AI chat wrapper
- A social media automation or bot tool
- A replacement for effort, thinking, or consistency
